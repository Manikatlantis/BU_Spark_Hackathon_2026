P.A.P.I — Point-cloud Analysis for Pathway Inclusion

P.A.P.I

Automated sidewalk hazard detection using high-density LiDAR data across Brookline, MA.

Raw LiDAR Geometry

Millions of 3D points captured by Cyvl.ai form the digital twin of sidewalks.

2 Meter Corridor Precision

Sidewalk centerlines are buffered in UTM coordinates to isolate the exact walkable surface.

Plane Fitting

z = ax + by + c

Least-squares regression estimates surface tilt from dense 3D point clouds.

Cross-Slope Formula

|a| / √(1 + a² + b²)

ADA maximum cross-slope: ≤ 2%.

Vertical Step Detection

Δz > 6mm flags cracks. Δz > 13mm classified as high severity hazards.

Impact

250 corridors analyzed. 32,467 hazards detected across Brookline.
